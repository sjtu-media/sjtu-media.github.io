SJTU MedIA

SJTU MedIA is based in the AGI Institute of the School of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, with research spanning biomedical image analysis, multimodal medical foundation models, and intelligent diagnosis.
The lab is led by Associate Professor Yi Hong.
