Ph.D. advisor, with a long-term research focus on medical image analysis and AI for smart healthcare. Recipient of the MICCAI 2014 Young Scientist Award, NSF CISE CRII Award, UNC Chapel Hill Ph.D. Completion Fellowship, Shanghai Overseas Leading Talent title, 2023 Shanghai Computer Society Teaching Achievement Award First Prize (2nd place), and 2024 Shanghai Jiao Tong University Teaching Achievement Award Second Prize (2nd place).
